Our Heritage

About Sirius Handloom

🧵

Woven from Tradition, Made for Today

Founded in 2004 in the coastal town of Maruthamunai, Sirius Handloom began as a small family workshop with a passion for preserving the ancient art of Sri Lankan handloom weaving.

Over two decades, we've grown into one of the island's most trusted handloom brands — yet our heart remains in the rhythm of the loom and the hands that operate it.

Every sarong we create carries the spirit of our artisans — men and women who have dedicated their lives to mastering a craft passed down through generations.

🌿

Our Commitment to Craftsmanship

We source only the finest 100% pure cotton yarns, dyed with vibrant, long-lasting colors. Every thread is hand-selected, every pattern hand-designed, and every sarong hand-woven on traditional pit looms.

No two pieces are exactly alike — that's not a flaw, it's the beauty of authentic handloom. When you own a Sirius sarong, you own a piece of living heritage.
